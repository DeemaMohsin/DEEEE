Our story

Our story began four months ago when we were thinking
about enrolling in the web development course for the new
semester at university. My friends and I decided to take it
together in the same section. When we enrolled, we found out
that the midterm was a project, and we were already aware of
this, so we decided to form a team. We came up with an
amazing idea, and after a week, we started designing to finish
everything, we scored a 30.
