Salted caramel latte

Recipe:

  • Make the cream:
    • Add cream, caramel sauce, a pinch of sea salt and 30ml milk.
    • Mix the all together.
  • Add ice to your cup.
  • Pour in caramel syrup.
  • Add 120ml milk of your choice.
  • Pour through a freshly brewed espresso.
  • Add your caramel cream on top.
