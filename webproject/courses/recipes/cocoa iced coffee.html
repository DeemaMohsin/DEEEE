Cacao iced coffee

Recipe:

  • Froth up 1 TBSP of instant coffee with 1/2 TBSP of sugar and a little bit of water.
  • Add ice and 30ml of milk.
  • Mix 1 TBSP of cocoa powder with 1/2 TBSP of sugar and a little bit of milk.
  • Pour trough the mixture.
