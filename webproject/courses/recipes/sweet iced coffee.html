Sweet iced coffee

Recipe:

  • Add to your cup:
    • 1 or 2 TBSP of instant coffee (depends on how strongyou'd like your coffee).
    • 1 or 2 TBSP of sugar (depending on how sweet you'd like it).
    • 120ml of water.
  • Froth for 1-2 minutes.
  • Add ice to your final cup.
  • Pour your sweet and foamy coffee on top.
  • Fill up your cup with your milk of choice.
