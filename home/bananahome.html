Banana cream iced coffee

Recipe:

  • Add ice to your cup.
  • Pour over your cold coffee.
  • Add some banana shake/cream on top.
  • Banana shake/cream:
  • Add to your shaker:
    • 1 banana.
    • Ice cubes.
    • 120ml milk of your choice.
  • Blend it till smooth.
